# Contributing directly to Hermit

First off, thank you for considering contributing to Hermit! It's people like you that make the open-source community such an amazing place to learn, inspire, and create.

## Legal & Licensing

**Hermit-AI, Inc.** requires that all contributors sign a Contributor License Agreement (CLA) before we can accept their pull requests.

### Why a CLA?
The CLA ensures that **Hermit-AI, Inc.** has the legal right to distribute your contribution and, if necessary, relicense it (e.g., for a commercial dual-license option for government/enterprise use). You retain copyright of your work, but grant us a license to use it.

By contributing to this repository, you certify that:
1. Your contribution is your own original work.
2. You have the right to submit it under the AGPL v3 license.
3. You agree to assign the necessary rights to Hermit-AI, Inc. for distribution.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the repo** and create your branch from `main`.
2. **Setup your environment**:
   ```bash
   ./setup.sh
   ```
3. **Make your changes**. Ensure your code follows the existing style (standard Python PEP 8).
4. **Test your changes**:
   ```bash
   python3 test_batch.py
   ```
5. **Add License Headers**: Ensure new files have the AGPL v3 header (see existing files).
6. **Submit a Pull Request**!
